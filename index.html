About Us

Established in 2018, Sweet House & Bliss is a cafe that combines the warmth of a traditional bakery with the concept of a "modern sweet" cafe. Located in the bustling city center, it has a decor that combines vintage elements with a sweet mix of modern concepts, creating an inviting atmosphere to relax and enjoy a meal.


Since 2020, Sweet House & Bliss has rapidly evolved into a cafe that offers a wide variety of food and beverages, from what used to be a regular bakery when it was first established. A wide variety of sweet cakes, pastries, drinks and main dishes such as spaghetti, chicken steak, etc., make up our menu with a variety of innovative races.

Vision

Presenting a unique experience for diners with a wide variety of many variations food and beverage, as well as a café aesthetic with a vintage shop flecked with a touch of modern sweet theme.
